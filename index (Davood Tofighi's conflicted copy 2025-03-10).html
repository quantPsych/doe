📖 Course Overview

This course introduces key principles of experimental design and analysis of variance (ANOVA) integrated with regression models. Key topics include:

  • Randomization, replication, and blocking principles
  • One-way and factorial ANOVA models
  • Managing unbalanced data and covariates
  • Advanced designs: Latin square, split-plot, nested, and mixed-effects models
  • Diagnostics: residual analysis, transformations, and multiple comparisons
